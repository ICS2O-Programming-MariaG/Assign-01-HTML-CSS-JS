So What is Climate Change?

The word climate, in general, refers to the average weather pattern of an area. Climate change is the change in the world's average weather pattern that has begun to occur as a result of the emission of greenhouse gases. Learn more about climate change here.

Why Does it Matter?

Climate change will have many negative effects on our environment and our lives. Below are some examples of the consequences of climate change. Please check off all the effects you have heard of before.

  • Wildfires
  • Drought
  • Flooding
  • More Frequent Severe Storms
  • Rising Sea Levels
