What Can You Do?

Now that you've learned about the consequences of climate change and what our government has pledged to do to fix the problem, you might be wondering how you can help. Luckily, individuals can help reduce the impacts of climate change! Keep reading to find out how you can make a difference...

Kinds of Actions:

As discussed on the second page, there are two different strategies for combatting climate change. Here is another brief summary of them:

Mitigation

Mitigation refers to actions of reducing carbon emissions to prevent the effects of climate change.
Learn more

Adaptation

Adaptation refers to changes we make in our behaviour and infrastructure to reduce the effects of climate change.
Learn more

Mitigation: What You Can Do

Here are some examples of what you can do to combat climate change with mitigation:

  1. Bike or walk instead of driving
  2. Reduce electricity consumption
  3. Plant a tree or garden
  4. Eat less red meat and dairy
  5. Buy local
  6. Look for eco-friendly products

Adaptation: What You Can Do

Below are some examples of what you can do to fight climate change with adaptation:

  1. Plant a tree or garden
  2. Clean out storm drains
  3. Grade your lot sloped away from your house
  4. Keep permeable ground in your yard
  5. Trim dead brush away from your house
  6. Plant drought-resistant plants
