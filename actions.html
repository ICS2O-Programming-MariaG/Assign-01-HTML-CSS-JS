What is Canada Doing?

Climate change is going to have terrible effects on our country and our world if we do nothing. But there's good news, too! Canada has a plan. So what is Canada doing to combat climate change?

Ways to Combat Climate Change

There are two main ways to combat climate change, and the Government of Canada is employing both of them. See below for a table of methods and examples:

Method Simple definition Examples
Mitigation Avoiding effects by reducing emissions Switching to electric vehicles
Avoiding using coal
Encouraging public transportation
Planting trees
Adaptation Reducing effects by changing behaviour
and infrastructure
 Increasing greenspace
Reducing water consumption
Creating bioswales
Maintenance of storm sewers
Sloping lots away from buildings

Canada's Plan

The Government of Canada plans to combat climate change using the following general strategies (and more):

  1. Sharing information about climate change
  2. Investing in infrastructure for adaptation
  3. Developing stricter building codes
  4. Addressing health effects as they occur
  5. Supporting Canada's northern and coastal communities
